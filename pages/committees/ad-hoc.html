WELCOME TO DUMUNC 2018
Ad-Hoc Committee of the Secretary-General

The Ad-Hoc Committee of the Secretary-General is the highest-level crisis committee at DUMUNC. Delegates will not be told of the topic or their positions until the start of committee. Fast-paced, realistic, and highly detailed crisis simulations will allow the most experienced delegates to showcase their skills.


There is no background guide for Ad-Hoc. Delegates need not write position papers for Ad-Hoc.
