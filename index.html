WELCOME TO DUMUNC 2018

Click here to register for DUMUNC XXXVI!


Dear Delegates and Faculty Advisors,

DUMUNC XXXVI will be held from Friday, February 16, to Sunday, February 18, 2018 in Durham, North Carolina. On behalf of the Duke International Relations Association, it is my privilege to invite you all to thirty-sixth session of the Duke University Model United Nations Conference. My staff and I are working diligently to craft the best possible Model United Nations experience for every delegate and advisor. We hope to continue a tradition of stimulating debate, fostering challenging discussions, and focusing on delegate engagement. As the premier Model UN conference in the South, we hope to provide a fantastic weekend filled with great experiences both in the committee rooms and through social events like our campus tours and delegate dance, all infused with some good, old-fashioned Southern hospitality.

More information in regards to specific committees, registration, and other conference logistics can be found throughout our website. If there are any questions or concerns between now and the date of the conference, please don't hesitate to reach out. We wish you the best in all of your yearly endeavors, and look forward to seeing you next February!

Sincerely,

Robert Steilberg
Secretary-General
